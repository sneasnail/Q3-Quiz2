The Little Mermaid

Synopsis

A rebellious 16-year-old mermaid named Ariel is fascinated with life on land. On one of her visits to the surface, which are forbidden by her controlling father, King Triton, she falls for a human prince named Eric.

Review

One of my favorite movies; The songs, vibrant animation and lively cast of characters are all iconic.---- 5 stars :)
★★★★★

Alice In Wonderland

Synopsis

Alice goes down a rabbit hole to find the mysterious underground Wonderland. She encounters fabulous creatures that defy all reasonable expectations. After numerous incoherent adventures involving a Hatter, a Hare and the Queen of Hearts, she wakes up in time for tea.

Review

Actual #1 Favorite Movie; The visuals and plot are mildly confusing and concerning-- they add to the charm.
★★★★★

The Lion King

Synopsis

Disney's The Lion King is about a young lion named Simba, who is the crown prince of an African Savanna. When his father dies in an accident staged by his uncle, Simba is made to feel responsible for his father's death and must overcome his fear of taking responsibility as the rightful heir to the throne.

Review

Outstanding animated feature from Disney that sports outstanding songs by Elton John, a compelling story and first-class animation.
★★★★★

Hercules

Synopsis

Disney tackles Greek mythology in this animated feature. Hercules, a son of gods, was snatched as a baby by Hades and forced to live among mortals as a half-man, half-god.

Review

Well structured with good characters and a good plot; (Mostly) Accurate to the legend, too. Hades is one of the best disney villains :)
★★★★★
